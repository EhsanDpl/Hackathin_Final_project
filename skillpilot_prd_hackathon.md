## Hackathon Goals

- **Learner (Software Engineer)**

  - Has limited time due to meetings and sprint work
  - Needs personalized learning instead of generic content

- **Mentor / Senior Engineer**
  - Provides occasional guidance
  - Needs visibility into learner progress
  - Wants structured checkpoints

- **Manager / OD Team**
  - Oversees employee growth
  - Needs progress visibility & skill insights
  - Must identify drop‚Äëoffs and bottlenecks
